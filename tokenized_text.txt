Azul fell -awen .
Ad as- t- id- awiɣ ma yebɣa -tent .
Si tama -nniḍen , axeddim -a ad d- yeglu s kra n ubaɣur meqqren i leqdic -nniḍen ara iḍerrun akka tura deg umaḍal umḍin .
Tirebbaɛ n usideg i yekkren i tmerna n teqbaylit ɣer tkarḍa tamaḍalant tillellit OpenStreetMap , ad izmiren ad sqedcen awalen -a ɣer tama n yismawen n tuddar , isaffen , iberdan , akken azekka ad naf taqbaylit deg wallalen am GPS , ladɣa akka tura imi yezmer kra n wagguren d asawen , ad nernu daɣen taɣect taqbayit .
Ittusemma , amdan ad yizmir ad isexdem GPS s teqbaylit xersum di tazwara di tmurt n Leqbayel .
Ass -a , tuget n yimdanen , seqdacen allalen atraren i yesseqdacen titiknulujiyin timaynutin am uselkim , tiliɣriyin .
Ɣef waya , acḥal d tikkelt ara d- neggar tiɣri i yimdanen akken ad suzuren s ṭaqa axeddim -nsen deg Internet , ad d- beṭṭun tiktiwin d tmussniwin -nsen i yilmeẓyen ara iqeddcen deg unnar n usideg n yifecka neɣ deg unnar n usemsel awurman n tutlayt .
Sin -a n yiḥricen ineggura , ad ǧǧen i yimdanen ad sqedcen imeẓla ama deg uselkim neɣ deg Internet s tutlayt -nsen tayemmat war ma terra -ten tmara ad smersen tutlayin tibeṛṛaniyin .
Deg taggara , ad snemmreɣ Aɣmis n Yimaziɣen i yettilin yal tikkelt deg unnar akken ad d- yawi isallen i icudden ɣer leqdic yezdin i tutlayt taqbaylit .
Ad snemmreɣ daɣen Mass Rabia Farid i yellan ɣef uqerru n tseqqamutt n usmuzzeɣ n twennaḍt tadeblant deg ugezdu n Tizi Uzzu , imi i aɣ- d- yefka tagnit akken ad d- nmudd ayen nessen , neɣ ad d- nerfed tamussni seg yimussnawen yellan deg unnar -a .
Nessaram ad d- ḍerrunt temliliyin am tigi s waṭas .
